About NAVVA+

About us

NAVVA+ was designed, developed, and created by Nawa.

This site was built as a focused tournament command experience for FIFA World Cup 2026 match tracking, group-stage understanding, venue mapping, and knockout-route visibility in one smart surface.

Product overview

NAVVA+ is a World Cup dashboard that brings together schedule visibility, group routing, interactive venue awareness, and knockout progression mapping in a single interface designed for fast reading and clear decision support.

Purpose: make the tournament easier to follow, understand, and monitor day by day.
